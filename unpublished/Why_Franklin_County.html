Important Franklin County Facts

  • Franklin County is the largest county in the state
  • Franklin County has a mix of rural and urban living
  • A major US Interstate runs through Franklin County
  • Many centers of employment are within an hour commute of Franklin County
  • Franklin Country is about an hour drive from Saint Louis International Airport

Summary

With its mix of home settings, its proximity to employment, shopping, entertainment and healthcare facilities Franklin County is an ideal place to live. It is well suited for young families, or seniors. There is something here for nearly everyone, and if it is not here, it is only a short drive away
